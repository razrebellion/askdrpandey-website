Infertility Care

Infertility is usually defined as not conceiving after a year of regular, unprotected intercourse (or six months if the woman is over 35). Many causes are treatable, and a clear evaluation is the first step.

Understanding the cause

Fertility involves both partners, so assessment considers ovulation, the fallopian tubes and uterus, and sperm health. Investigations are chosen based on your individual history.

Treatment options

Depending on the findings, options range from lifestyle and ovulation support to procedures such as IUI. Dr. Pandey explains each option clearly so you can make informed decisions.

A supportive approach

Fertility care can be an emotional journey. The aim is to provide honest guidance and steady support at every step.
